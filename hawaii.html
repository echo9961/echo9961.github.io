Hawaii

The archipelego many dream of visiting

When did you visit Hawaii?

I visited Hawaii with my family back in 2017 for summer vacation. They decided we would visit the island of Kauai since it was the island they went to last time for their trip to Hawaii. They really wanted me to see that part of Hawaii since it has more greenery than the other islands in that archipelego.

I was there for about a week, and I'd have to say it was pretty fun over there. There were plenty of things for me to do over there in Kauai.

What did you do over in Hawaii?

There were plenty of things for me to do over in Hawaii (probably a little too much to count). Nevertheless, I will attempt to describe any many things as possible for what I did in Hawaii.

One of the things I did in Hawaii was go snorkelling. Thanks to that, I realized that I could enjoy swimming with snorkeling gear due to getting a near-constant flow of oxygen. Another thing I did over there was go through a zipline course. That course had different ziplines set at various heights and lengths. I did attend a luau in Hawaii, which was fairly festive as others have described at times. A constant thing we did over in Hawaii was visit a local market that had a Poke Bowl stand and a Smoothie Stand (both of them were pretty fresh).

Is there anything else you want to say about Hawaii?

Hawaii is definitely worth visiting at some point. I would recommend doing your research on what Island you want to visit before going. After all, traveling there by plane is pretty expensive. Plus you need to make sure you know where you're going and what you're going to do so you can handle your finances more efficiently over there.

Other than that, I would recommend going to Hawaii for a trip. Like I said earlier, make sure your finances are planned for it as well as your schedule. If anyone reading this plans on visiting Hawaii, I hope your trip there is fun.
