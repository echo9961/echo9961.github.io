History of Music In My Life

Life With Music Can be More than Just Listening to It

Playing Trumpet

One of the things involving music that I have done is play trumptet. When I was in elementary school, I decided to start playing trumpet in the school concert band. I had a decent knack for it apparently. From that point on, I continued to partipate in having school concert band as my electives in school.

That kept going on until I graduated from high school. After I graduated high school, I decided to step away from playing music in order to see what I really wanted to do in life. But I will say it was fun going through school events for concert band up until the end of high school.

Marching Band

Throughout High School, I participated in marching band since it was part of the school band curriculum. That gave me some experience with how teams handled music in more than 1 genre. I will admit, it was definitely different than how concert band usually is.

For all 4 years of high school, marching band was an engaging experience since it kept me busy throughout high school. Honestly, it was either that or end up in High School P.E. instead. Nonetheless, I admit it was a fun time.

Music in the Car

Ever since I got my Driver's License in high school, I often listen to music in my car. That often comes from my phone or an MP3 player whenever I have the opportunity to have it play in the car.

More often then not, I'm usually playing video game music that I save on my phone. Some of it is either good for background music or if I just want to listen to a neat song that's stuck in my brain.
